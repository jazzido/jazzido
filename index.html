Open Source Software

Tabula
http://github.com/jazzido/tabula

Tabula is a tool for liberating data tables embedded in PDF files. I started the project in late 2012 and it quickly generated a lot of interest in the data journalism and open data communities. It is now being developed in collaboration with ProPublica. Available under the MIT License.

Gasto Público Bahiense
http://github.com/jazzido/GPB

Launched in mid-2010, Gasto Público Bahiense scrapes and republishes the expenditure data published by the government of Bahía Blanca, Argentina, my hometown. It is one of the first grassroots projects of technology applied to government transparency in Latin America. In 2013, the government of the Buenos Aires province awarded it the annual "Innovation in Government" prize. Available under a GPLv3 license.
